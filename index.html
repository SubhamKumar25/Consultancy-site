About Sanvi Global Consultancy

Founded in 2008, Sanvi Global Consultancy has been a trusted partner for students aspiring to pursue education in India and abroad. Our team of experienced counselors and education experts are dedicated to helping you navigate the complex world of higher education.

Our Mission

To empower students with accurate information, personalized guidance, and comprehensive support to make informed decisions about their education and career paths.

Our Approach

  • Personalized Attention: One-on-one counseling tailored to each student's needs
  • Transparent Process: Clear communication at every step of the journey
  • Ethical Practices: Honest advice with the student's best interest at heart
  • End-to-End Support: From initial counseling to post-arrival assistance
